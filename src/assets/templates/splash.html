Students: Learn how CalCentral can save you time, notify you of critical status changes, and help you stay on top of your academic, administrative and campus life activities.

Enrollments and Wait List: The Classes card in My Academics provides students with a list of their current enrollments, including wait list status.

Tele-BEARS and GPA Calculator: The Appointments card in My Academics shows students their Tele-BEARS appointments, as well as whether or not they need an advisor code beforehand. The GPA Calculator is pre-configured with a student's classes and units, making it easy to calculate a possible GPA for the semester.

bConnected and Academic Status: bConnected apps are easy to access from any page on CalCentral. Badges on the icons show unread bMail messages and new invitations to bCal events. Academic Status (person icon) shows important registration and other status.

My Finances: The new consolidated financial view (CFV) in My Finances provides a student with detailed transaction information on his/her CARS billing account (ex: charges, payments and disbursements). My Finances shows recent activity since the last monthly CARS e-Bill statement.
